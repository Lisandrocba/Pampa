About us

We are a company made up of foreign trade professionals, dedicated to the commercial development of customers, markets and suppliers of inputs as well as finished products.

At HPampa we have the backing of more than 18 years of experience from TEBSA S.R.L., an Argentine company with more than 100 participations in missions and international fairs in more than 30 countries.

We are located in the province of Zaragoza, Spain and we have operational bases in Argentina, Brazil, Uruguay, the United States and China.

  • Development of suppliers of products and / or supplies
  • Production control in foreign suppliers
  • Sending samples to foreign clients
  • Export on behalf and order of third parties
  • Participation in own and inverse missions
  • Promotion through own merchants
  • Currency entry, exit and settlement
  • Preparation and control of documents
  • Procedures in the registry of importers / exporters
  • Processing of certificates and authorizations
  • Advice on the mode of transport
  • Physical supervision of shipments
  • Coordination and monitoring of shipment / destination
  • Track export refunds
  • Tax and customs analysis
  • Preparation and presentation of SIMI and LNA
  • Planning and generation of purchase orders
  • Management of pre-import certifications
  • Control of temporary imports
  • Typification of products with temporary inputs
